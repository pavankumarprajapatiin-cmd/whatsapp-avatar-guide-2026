What Is the WhatsApp Avatar Feature? And How to Create Best Avatar in WhatsApp
WhatsApp Avatar is a built-in customization tool that lets users create a digital character that represents them. Once created, WhatsApp automatically generates multiple expressive stickers based on your avatar.

According to official updates from Meta Platforms (WhatsApp’s parent company), avatars are designed to give users more personalized ways to express themselves in chats without sharing real photos.

You can use your avatar to:

Set as a profile picture
Send as stickers
Use in chats and reactions
Update anytime
Unlike third-party apps, this feature works directly inside WhatsApp. No extra downloads are required.

Step-by-Step: How to Create Avatar in WhatsApp (Android)
Follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Open WhatsApp
Launch the latest version of WhatsApp on your Android phone.

Step 2: Go to Settings
Tap the three-dot menu in the top right corner and select Settings.

Step 3: Select Avatar
Inside settings, you’ll see the Avatar option (if your app is updated).

Step 4: Tap “Create Your Avatar”
Start building your digital character.

Step 5: Customize Your Look
You can choose:

Skin tone
Hairstyle
Face shape
Eye color
Eyebrows
Outfit
Accessories
Step 6: Save
Tap Done, and WhatsApp will generate your avatar sticker pack automatically.

That’s it. Your avatar is ready.
How to Create Avatar in WhatsApp (iPhone)
The process on iPhone is very similar:

Open WhatsApp
Tap Settings (bottom right corner)
Select Avatar
Tap Create Your Avatar
Customize features
Save
WhatsApp automatically creates a full sticker set for you.
